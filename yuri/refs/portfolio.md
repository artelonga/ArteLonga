A network of web services developed by **ArteLonga** — it builds **co** and distributes the whole network to whoever arrives. Each one is live; hover any node in the map above to see what it is and what it's built with.

**[co](https://co.artelonga.com.br) — the shared home**
Where communities write, organize and publish together: boards, wikis and living gardens, updated as you go. Sign in once, get one bill, and everything else here comes included.

**[yggdrasil](https://yggdrasil.artelonga.com.br) — web-based game engine**
A Godot 4 / WASM game engine in the browser — games and interactive experiences that open in a tab, nothing to install.

**[neuroanatomy](https://yggdrasil.artelonga.com.br/static/anatomia/) — learn the brain by playing**
A playful way to explore the brain — its parts and how they fit. A universe of its own, sibling to yggdrasil, that draws on **neuro**.

**comunicação — language through culture and history**
Learn to speak [Mbyá Guarani](https://yggdrasil.artelonga.com.br/universos/comunicacao?id=public-mbya) or [Yorùbá](https://yggdrasil.artelonga.com.br/universos/comunicacao?id=public-yoruba) through play — ancestral languages kept alive as cultural impact, tied to **Quilombo Araucária**.

**[Quilombo Araucária](https://quilomboaraucaria.org) — social impact**
A community tied to ArteLonga through **social impact** — a space of environmental, cultural and social resistance. Living archive: stories, knowledge and photos, kept alive.

**[rfq](https://rfq.artelonga.com.br) — instant pricing**
Ask for a price on a financial trade and get one back immediately, worked out live — in partnership with the **[Hedix](https://hedix.com.br/)** market-maker.

**[neuro](https://neuro.artelonga.com.br) — neuroscience bibliography, together**
A public, collaborative repository of neuroscience references — built with **Neuro Notebook Brasil**. Browse the bibliography and add your own.

**[ArteLonga](https://artelonga.com.br) — the public home**
Who we are, the people behind the agency, and what we offer.

Everything signs in and stores through **co**: **one set of credentials, one bill, every service available to anyone who arrives.** All of it runs in São Paulo — hosted, with DNS, on **Hostinger**.

### Why this maps to the Data Analyst role

Each chapter of the timeline is a skill this role asks for — on top of years as a data professional ([full history in the résumé](/yuri/resume/)):

- **ArteLonga → co** — owning data end-to-end: identity, billing and storage at scale. Building the systems that *produce* the data an analyst reads.
- **rfq** — quantitative modeling on live data: market making for prediction markets. Probabilistic thinking, real-time signals.
- **yggdrasil & comunicação** — turning data into interactive, understandable experiences. Communicating insight, multilingual.
- **neuro** — open data and rigor: a public bibliography, research-grade sourcing, NLP/ML.
- **Infra as a Service** — the cost and scale of data infrastructure — the platform an analyst depends on.
- **the numbers** — shipping velocity: 1,291 commits, 274 PRs, ~360k lines, solo, in production.

Before ArteLonga: **Senior Data Scientist** (retail analytics on Spark/Delta Lake), **Quant**, and **research** (data acquisition, statistics, ML). I come to Hostinger already an **avid user** — DNS and hosting for everything above — wanting to join as a **technical member**.
